Holland Lop

Holland Lops are the smallest of the lop-eared rabbits, smaller than lops and even mini lops.

  • Scientific Name: Oryctolagus cuniculus
  • Origin or Range: Netherlands
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Compatibility: Relatively Non-Aggressive

Holland Lops have a wonderful temperament, especially compared to other rabbits their size. Curious and relatively easily trained, they are fun to watch and fun to work with.
